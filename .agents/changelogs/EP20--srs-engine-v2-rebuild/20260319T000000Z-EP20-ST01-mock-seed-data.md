- Defined `MockConsonant` interface: `id`, `native`, `romanization` (consonant name), `english` (sound value), `class`, `language`

- Defined `MockWord` interface: `native`, `romanization` (phonetic), `english`, `type`, `language`
- 15 curated Thai words: 12 from "let's eat something", 3 from "weather is hot today"

## Behavior Preserved / New Behavior

- No engine logic — pure static data
- `romanization` on consonants = consonant name (e.g. `Ko Kai`), not phonetic
- `romanization` on words = phonetic pronunciation (e.g. `hǐw`)
